# XDC FuelCheck NSW

**Real-time NSW fuel prices with your personal discounts — now with XDC and stablecoin pricing.**

XDC FuelCheck is a simple tool that shows you the cheapest fuel prices near you in New South Wales, based on official government data. You can add your own discounts (like NRMA, work fuel cards, or loyalty programs) and even see what the price would be if you paid in **XDC** or **USDC** (on the XDC Network).

---

## What is it?

XDC FuelCheck NSW helps you find the best fuel prices in New South Wales. It shows live prices from petrol stations and lets you:

- See the cheapest stations near you on a map
- Add your personal discounts so you see your **actual** price
- View prices in **XDC** or **USDC** (useful if you use cards that let you spend crypto)

It’s designed to be simple and useful for everyday drivers.

---

## How to Use It

1. **Open the site**  
   Go to: [https://s4njk4n.github.io/XDC_FuelCheck/](https://s4njk4n.github.io/XDC_FuelCheck/)

2. **Choose your fuel type**  
   Select U91, E10, Diesel, P95, or P98 at the top.

3. **Look at the map**  
   The map shows petrol stations in your current view. Zoom and move around to see different areas.

4. **Check the ranked list**  
   Below the map, you’ll see the cheapest stations currently visible on the map (sorted from cheapest to most expensive).

5. **Add your discounts** (optional but useful)
   - Scroll down to **"Your Discounts"**
   - Select a brand (e.g. United, Ampol, Shell, etc.) - Only service station brands visible on the map are shown in the dropdown list
   - Enter how many cents per litre cheaper you get
   - Add a note (e.g. “NRMA”, “Work card”, “Woolies”)
   - Click **Save Discount**

   Once saved, your discounted price will automatically appear in the list.

6. **View prices in XDC or USDC** (optional)
   At the bottom, use the **"Display Prices In"** dropdown to switch between:
   - AUD (normal dollars)
   - XDC
   - USDC (on the XDC Network)

   This is handy if you use payment cards that let you spend XDC or stablecoins.

7. **Save to your phone** (recommended)
   On mobile, tap the share button and choose **“Add to Home Screen”**. It will work like a normal app.

## How It Works

XDC FuelCheck pulls live fuel price data directly from the **NSW Government’s official Fuel API**. This means the prices you see are the same ones published by the state government.

The tool also lets you see what those prices would cost in **XDC** or **USDC** (a stablecoin on the XDC Network). This is especially useful for people who use crypto payment cards, as it gives them a quick way to compare real-world spending power.

All your discounts are saved privately in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.
